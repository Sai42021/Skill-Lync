About Us

What we do

SKILL LYNC provides university and college students with the opportunity to earn an income. Tutors are active university students looking for alternative income streams. Skill Lync provides a platform for tutors to connect and set up appointments with clients. Tutors can attract customers, and parents can easily find tutors using Skill Lync.

Mission

We seek to create a platform that not only creates
income generating opportunities,
for unemployed youth but also
makes these opportunities easy to reach.

Goals

  • Reach nation-wide markets.
  • Provide Income generating opportunities to millions nation-wide
  • Implement a user-friendly interface.
  • Leverage emerging technologies
